How We Can Help

You sell on multiple channels and we manage product data feeds for all of them. We customize product data to show your customers the right information based on the channel serving ads.

Product catalog research

Your products fill a specific niche in the market. We identify the keywords shoppers use to find products like yours and add them to your feed where shoppers see them.

Industry-specific data

Your products require specialized feed attributes to give customers the info. they’re look for. We add every relevant attribute to your product feed to improve visibility.

Product category research

Your business sells products in a dynamic market to customers with changing tastes. We research trends in customer searches to plan ads effectively.

Accurate product categorization

Shoppers and search engines locate products according to their categories, sub-categories and more. We’ll include this in your product data for search optimization and reporting.

Feed driven text ads

With optimized product data feed, we deploy text ads for each item in your product catalog.

Feed driven display ads

We fill each display, Discovery and Performance Max ad placement with optimal product images and text.
