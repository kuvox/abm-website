Supported Ad Platforms

Search, Social, Marketplaces & AI

Channels We Manage

We’re steadily learning new ad channels to help our clients reach customers as traffic migrates from Google to LLMs and social platforms.

Most of our work stays on Google Ads and product feeds, but we extend to the channels below when they’ll increase overall sales and profitability.

Google & YouTube

Google Shopping ads are the most important ad surface for most e-commerce stores — and they’re entirely driven by product feed quality. We also run Google Search and Display to capture incremental intent, and YouTube to reach buyers on the second-most-visited site in the US. See our services and pricing for how we manage these channels.

Microsoft

Carry your success on Google and Amazon over to Microsoft Ads, where you can reach customers on Bing, AOL, Yahoo, and DuckDuckGo. Microsoft search traffic represents 10–15% of Google’s, yet it can deliver cheaper clicks and a higher-spending audience for the right verticals. See our services and pricing for how we manage Microsoft campaigns.

ChatGPT

Product data feeds are the engine behind product ads in Google search results — and that same structured catalog data is increasingly how LLMs like ChatGPT surface products and brands to shoppers. We optimize feeds so your inventory is ready for AI-driven discovery as well as traditional search.

Meta

Our product feed work transfers to social catalog ads with little extra effort. We help clients run Facebook and Instagram campaigns that match the right products to shopper interests using optimized titles, attributes, and audience signals from your store data.

Pinterest

Pinterest is a strong discovery channel for visual and lifestyle products. With a clean product feed, we extend your catalog to Pinterest shopping ads so pinners who are planning purchases can find your SKUs alongside your Google and social campaigns.

Target

Retail marketplace ads put your products in front of shoppers already browsing Target.com. We help brands structure feeds and campaigns for marketplace placements where catalog scale and accurate product data determine whether your offers show up in relevant results.

Walmart

Walmart’s retail media network reaches high-intent shoppers on one of the largest US marketplaces. We support Walmart ad setup and feed-driven campaigns for brands that want another scalable sales channel beyond Google and Amazon.

Reddit

Reddit ads reach niche communities with strong purchase intent in categories like tools, automotive, hobbies, and industrial supply. We test Reddit alongside search and social when your audience congregates in subreddits related to your products.

What it’s Like to Work With Us

We work closely with our clients to understand their growth plans and growth limits. That allows both your team and ours to aim for ambitious goals that are feasible and within your budget.
