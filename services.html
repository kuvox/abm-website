Services + Pricing

Google Shopping Plus Social & Ai Ads

Ad Channels We Support

Approximately 80% of our work is on Google Ads and shopping data feeds. That means we’re the right fit for large SKU-count retailers and brands that generate between $5M and $50M in annual revenue.

We support other ad channels when we know that they’ll increase our clients’ overall sales and profitability.

Ad Management

This is the best way for us to grow your business: a long-term strategy and implementation role. We manage everything from preparing product data, giving feedback on conversion-killing website bugs, placing and optimizing your ads, all the way through conversion tracking. Of course, since we specialize in Shopify, we’re able to dramatically improve your conversion data via tracking code implementation that links all of Shopify’s data to all of Google Ads’ conversion events. The same is true for Microsoft, YouTube and all other channels we manage for you.

Product Feeds

Product data feed optimization is our strongest skillset. We’ve published articles on the DataFeedWatch.com blog (this is our preferred data feed management software), and Austin posts data feed optimization tips and tutorials regularly on the YouTube channel PPC for Everyone.

Our team takes special care of all our clients’ product data feeds because we know from experience that one of the surest ways to increase sales in online shopping ads is to have at or near 100% of your inventory approved and eligible to show ads at all times.

Consulting + Training

Our team provides one-time consulting and training on an hourly basis. Consulting engagements we provide can cover topics like: improving conversion tracking data, structuring ad account campaigns, and competing in the search ad auction within our familiar industries (industrial and replacement parts, automotive, apparel, vehicle dealerships).

In particular, we provide consulting on product data feed optimization, data cleanliness, and data management. We make product data feed management efficient to save precious work hours from being consumed by data woes.

Large Shop: % of attributed revenue on $45k to $65k per month ad spend on Google and Microsoft with a 100k+ SKU catalog.

Medium Shop: Flat fee on $25k to $55k per month total ad spend on Google, Microsoft, Pinterest and Meta. Extra support on graphic and video creative assets.

Real Fee Examples

Our fees depend on the complexity of your product catalog, website, and on the size of your ad budget. A large ad budget doesn’t necessarily mean a large service fee.

Our minimum flat fee is typically $1,500 per month. Regardless of your ad budget, please inquire so we can A) see if we’re a fit and B) set a service fee that’s feasible for both you and for us.

What it’s Like to Work With Us
